## Timing & Frequency

### Onboarding Paywall
- **When**: Before first grid view
- **Frequency**: Once
- **Goal**: Convert to Plus immediately

### Limit Paywalls
- **When**: At limit or feature access
- **Frequency**: Every time
- **Goal**: Convert frustrated users

### Grid Upsells
- **When**: Every 20-30 profiles
- **Frequency**: Not more than 1 per 15 profiles
- **Goal**: Awareness + IAP conversions

### Post-Match Upsells
- **When**: After matches
- **Frequency**: Every 3-5 matches
- **Goal**: Capitalize on positive emotion

### Settings Upgrades
- **When**: User views settings
- **Frequency**: Always visible
- **Goal**: Passive reminder + comparison
